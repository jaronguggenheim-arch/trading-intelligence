The solution

Every edge. In plain English.

Insider Cluster Buys

When multiple executives buy their own company stock in a short window, it signals unusual conviction from people with the best information.

"3 ASML executives bought $2.4M with their own money this week — that's rare and historically bullish."
🌊

Dark Pool Prints

Off-exchange block trades that institutions use to move large positions without tipping off the market. Big prints often precede big moves.

"$340M dark pool block printed on NVDA at $870 — above current ask, suggesting accumulation."
⚡

Options Flow

Large, sweeping call or put orders that are too big to ignore. Unusual options activity often front-runs catalysts by days or weeks.

"$2.1M call sweep on PLTR, 45-day expiry — far above average daily volume. Someone expects a move."
📊

Analyst Upgrades

Institutional analyst revisions from top-tier banks carry weight, especially when price targets move significantly above consensus.

"Goldman upgraded ASML to Strong Buy, raising PT from $720 → $850. 5th analyst upgrade this quarter."
🧮

GEX / Gamma Exposure

Market maker gamma positioning reveals where price is likely to be pinned — or where it could accelerate dramatically.

"NVDA flipped to positive GEX at $880. Dealers are now long gamma — price acceleration likely above this level."
🔗

Supply Chain Correlation

When a key supplier moves, the downstream stocks often follow within days. We map 6 chains so you can see the ripple before it hits.

"ASML booked record lithography orders. NVDA, TSM, and SMCI historically follow with 3–7 day lag."
